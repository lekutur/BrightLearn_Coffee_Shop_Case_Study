The coffee shop data was collected for 6 months, starting from January to June from 3 store. The objective is to analyse the data and see the sales report for different products and use the results to make sound business decisions. I used the time buckets to get to the sales period by product category and details, compared the 3 stores for the period of 6 months, and was able to retrieve the following reports:

1.Revenue generation per store location:
•	All store locations had an increase in the sale of coffee from March to June, reason being the change in seasons as clients drink more coffee in winter.
•	Hell’s Kitchen had the highest sale throughout the 6 months, while Astoria and Lower Manhattan are interchanging.

2.Sales count per month:
•	More sales happened during the weekdays as compared to weekends.

3. Total percentage sales by store
•	Astoria and Hell’s Kitchen sold 34% each while Lower Manhattan sold 32%

5. Monthly sales per product detail:
•	Lower Manhattan sold more than the 2 store
•	Jamaican coffee river and I need my bean diner mug are the least sold product at all stores

7. Monthly sales per product details
•	Loose tea, packaged chocolate and branded are the least sold products

I used CANVA, MIRO, Excel and Databrick for my project.

The final results was handed over to the CEO for sound business decicion making
